Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

A well-liked tourist destination in the Sri Lankan town of Hikkaduwa is the Turtle Hatchery. It is a project for conservation that aims to safeguard sea turtles and their eggs while also educating people about the value of these animals to the health of the marine ecosystem. The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery's main goal is to safeguard sea turtles from numerous dangers and provide a secure environment for their eggs to hatch. The hatchery gathers turtle eggs that are at risk of theft or predation and takes good care of them until they hatch. The newborn turtles are released into the water when they have emerged.And guided educational tours are available for hatchery visitors.
